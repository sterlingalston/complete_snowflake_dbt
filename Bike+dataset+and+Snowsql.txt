*** Bike dataset and Snowsql ***

- Link for the citibike schema: https://citibikenyc.com/system-data

- Link for downloading the citibike datasets: https://s3.amazonaws.com/tripdata/index.html

- Link to download Snowsql: https://www.snowflake.com/en/developers/downloads/snowsql/

- Command to automate the iteration of extraction of zipped folders:
	
	- Open Powershell (or the terminal on Mac) and navigate to where the unzipped parent folder is
	
	- On Powershell (Windows) execute the command:
	
		Get-ChildItem -Path . -Recurse -Filter *.zip | ForEach-Object { Expand-Archive -Path $_.FullName -DestinationPath ($_.DirectoryName + "\" + $_.BaseName) -Force }


	- Alternatively on Mac Terminal execute the command:
	
		find . -name "*.zip" -exec sh -c ' for zip; do dir="${zip%.zip}" mkdir -p "$dir" && unzip -o "$zip" -d "$dir" done' _ {} +
