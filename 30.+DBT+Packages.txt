

- Link to dbt packages hub: https://hub.getdbt.com/

- Link to dbt_utils package documentation: https://hub.getdbt.com/dbt-labs/dbt_utils/latest/


- At the root level of your project, create a packages.yml file and paste:


***


packages:
  - package: dbt-labs/dbt_utils
    version: 1.3.0

***

- Then in your terminal, you can run:

		dbt deps
